about me

In spring 2021, I graduated from Florida State University with my MA in Digital Humanities. My thesis is titled "'Machines Certainly Think': Generating Surrealist Text with Artificial Intelligence' and was published in Issue 4 of Avant-garde Studies, an online publication associated with The Dali Museum in St. Petersburg, Florida. In May of 2019, I earned my BA from FSU. I double majored in English and Humanities with a minor in Film Studies. My interests and hobbies include writing, editing, graphic design, art, photography, film, data, and artificial intelligence. My most recent professional experience in graphic design is my internship at The Dali Museum in St. Petersburg, Florida during the summer of 2017.
