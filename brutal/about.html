In spring 2021, I graduated from Florida State University with my MA in Digital Humanities. My most recent experience in graphic design is my internship at The Dali Museum in St. Petersburg, Florida during the summer of 2017. I have completed a handful of projects since then, as my class schedule has taken up most of my time. Please navigate to my contact page to reach me and discuss potential projects.
